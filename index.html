Our Mission

The United States’s tense social climate, stemming from cultural intolerance and ignorance, has greatly disrupted national unity since the nation’s foundation. UniteUS combats the volatility of the situation by encouraging cultural education and advancing community consolidation nationwide through various strategies that are viewable on our website. See "Our Strategies" below.

As a diverse team ourselves, we at UniteUS greatly value the importance of respecting individuals of all backgrounds and learning to understand one another in spite of our differences. It is for this reason that we created this platform: to recognize, celebrate, and appreciate the beautiful diversity within the United States.

Our Strategies

Through replacements of outdated statues and modern installations; daily online presentations of influential individuals from diverse backgrounds; and local community support through the promotion of diverse business owners, UniteUS strives to facilitate social good now and far into the future.
