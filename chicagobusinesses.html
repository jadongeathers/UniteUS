Chicago Culturally-Linked Businesses

Below is a directory of minority-owned businesses in Chicago:

Ain't She Sweet Cafe

  • Owner's Ethnicity: Black
  • Address: 526 E 43rd St #2920, Chicago, IL 60653
  • Description: This small, welcoming cafe features a comfortable atmosphere for its customers to enjoy a tasty selection of smoothies, sandwiches, salads, and desserts.

Virtue Restaurant

  • Owner's Ethnicity: Black
  • Address: 1462 E 53rd St, Chicago, IL 60615
  • Description: The Virtue Restaurant promises its customers with the greatest quality of service, coupled with comforting vibes of hospitality. This restaurant features a delicious Southern American cuisine.

Ina Mae Tavern & Packaged Goods

  • Owner's Ethnicity: Black
  • Address: 1415 N Wood St, Chicago, IL 60622
  • Description: This restaurant prides itself on its New Orleans vibe, presenting to its customers a variety of Southern-styled food developed by Executive Chef Brian Jupiter.

American Indian Center

  • Owner's Ethnicity: American Indian
  • Address: 3401 W Ainslie St, Chicago, IL 60625
  • Description: As stated in its mission statement, the American Indian Center exists to, "promote fellowship among Indian people of all Tribes living in metropolitan Chicago and to create bonds of understanding and communication between Indians and non-Indians in this city."

The National Museum of Puerto Rican Arts and Culture

  • Owner's Ethnicity: Puerto Rican
  • Address: 3015 W Division St, Chicago, IL 60622
  • Description: This museum's aim is to promote and educate teh public on the beauty and richness of Puerto Rican culture, thus serving as an integral center for cultural learning.

Mr. Brown's Lounge

  • Owner's Ethnicity: Black
  • Address: 81 E Wacker Pl, Chicago, IL 60601
  • Description: Mr. Brown's Lounge serves as an ideal spot to indulge in Carribean-styled food and drinks in a casual, comfortable setting.

Frontier Chicago

  • Owner's Ethnicity: Black
  • Address: 1072 N Milwaukee Ave, Chicago, IL 60642
  • Description: With a menu developed by talented chef Brian Jupiter, Frontier Chicago promises a delicious experience with its food and beers made in unique fashion, paying tribute to life of the old American West.
